ABOUT US

Welcome to Gurukul International School & Junior College, located in Malad-West, Mumbai, has been a beacon of quality education since its establishment in June 2006. Offering both CBSE and State Board syllabi, our institution caters to students from nursery to class 12th, providing comprehensive education in both commerce and science streams. We take pride in our commitment to nurturing students not only academically but also by equipping them with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in today's competitive world.
